Hand Circular Saw

This power tool uses a round metal blade edged with sharp teeth to cut an array of material such as woods, metals, cement block, brick, fiberglass, plastics and slate.

Characteristics

  1. Cutting is done with an abrasive wheel or teeth on the edge of a metal blade.
  2. The surface finish is relatively smooth, and the cut has a small kerf only.
  3. Cuts are relatively accurate and straight.
  4. Burrs are frequently left behind by the saw on the edges of metal and plastic, which should be removed with sandpaper.
  5. It is best to arrange the saw in a geometric manner.

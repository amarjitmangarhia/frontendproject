HandyMan Saw

What we do... ?

HandyMan Saw is company founded in 1909 by Robert Sawman. He was the man who was always curious about new tools. In past, cutting heavy metals and iron was a very hefty task, he want to make this easier by building a sharp and automatic tool. Hence he started HandyMan Saw company and make many people's life easier.
